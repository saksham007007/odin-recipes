Shahi Paneer

Shahi paneer is a preparation of paneer in a creamy curry sauce. Native to the Indian subcontinent, it has a thick gravy with cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.[1] The dish is prepared by emulsifying tomatoes, onions, ground cashews, ghee (clarified butter), and cream into a smooth, rich curry , which is then simmered with cubes of paneer and a blend of aromatic spices. Similar dishes includepaneer butter masala and kadai paneer. Paneer butter masala differs from shahi paneer in that whole spices are used in paneer butter masala, whereas shahi paneer generally has a sweeter taste in comparison.

INGREDIENTS

  • Paneer
  • Base and gravy
  • Aromatics
  • Tomtoes
  • Onions

STEPS

  1. Prepare the base
  2. Blend and Sieve the gravy
  3. Cook the gravy
  4. Add the paneer and finish
